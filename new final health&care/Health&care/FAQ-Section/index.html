Q. What all should I carry for my first appointment?

Please bring a responsible attendant and all your health related and medication related documents. Please also indicate if you have any allergies. Health insurance information, if you have any copies of policies along with.

Q. Is it possible for a first time patient to visit health&care without any medical reference?

Absolutely yes! health&care is more like a home equipped with best healthcare facilities. You can start your treatment process without any prior reference. However, sharing the medical history with the physician is a good idea. It is the best way to help your treatment process.

Q. In what ways Health&Care maintains the quality parameters in bed allotment and medical facilities ?

When it comes to quality, we take it as a necessity than just an option! Maintaining quality in equipment, professionals, facilities, costs and ethics is a healthy concern for Indus. Indus hospitals have dedicated staff for facilitating quality management requirements in the hospital premise.
